I'll be honest — I resisted Notion for a long time. Another all-in-one tool promising to fix my productivity? Sure.

But after actually using it for a few months across client projects, I get it now.

## What I Use It For

Notion became my go-to for client onboarding docs, project wikis, and content planning. The database feature alone replaced two other tools I was paying for.

## The Learning Curve Is Real

Don't expect to open Notion and immediately feel productive. The first week I kept building things wrong and starting over. The second week, something clicked.

## Bottom Line

If you're managing multiple projects or running a business solo, Notion is worth a serious look. Start with a template, don't try to build everything at once, and give it 2–3 weeks before you judge it.
