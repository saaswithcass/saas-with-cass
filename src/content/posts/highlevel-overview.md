**What's the difference between HighLevel and HubSpot?**
HubSpot is more complex and enterprise-focused, with higher pricing. HighLevel is designed specifically for agencies and offers white-label capabilities that HubSpot doesn't.

**Can I white-label HighLevel?**
Yes, that's one of its core features. You can rebrand it entirely and resell it to clients.

**Does HighLevel have a free plan?**
No permanent free plan, but there's a 14-day free trial. After that, paid plans start at $99/month.

**Can I integrate HighLevel with other tools?**
Yes, HighLevel integrates with many popular platforms including Zapier, webhooks, and native integrations with email providers, payment processors, and more.

**Is HighLevel good for freelancers?**
It can be, but it's designed primarily for agencies managing multiple clients. Solo freelancers might find simpler alternatives more cost-effective.

**How long does it take to set up?**
Basic setup takes 1-2 hours. Full implementation with all features can take longer depending on complexity.

**What kind of customer support does HighLevel offer?**
Email, live chat, and video support. Response times are typically fast, and the team is knowledgeable.

**Can I host landing pages on my own domain?**
Yes, you can use custom domains for all landing pages and websites built in HighLevel.

**Does HighLevel handle e-commerce?**
Yes, it has e-commerce capabilities including shopping carts and payment processing.

**What's included in the white-label feature?**
Everything—your clients log in and see your branding instead of "HighLevel." You control the pricing and resell it as your own platform.
